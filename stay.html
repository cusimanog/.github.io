Westin Palace Hotel

Plaza de las Cortes, 7, 28014 Madrid, Spain

This luxury hotel is located in the heart of Madrid, within the Art Triangle. The Parliament is across the road, and the Royal Palace and Opera House within walking distance. The Westin Palace offers elegant facilities only walking distance from from the Prado, Thyssen and Reina Sofia Museums (Kemper, 2018). The hotel is famous for its beautiful stained-glass dome. Here a fantastic buffet breakfast with music is served.

Kemper, Benjamin. (2018, June 7). Westin Palace Madrid: Hotel Review. Retrieved from Fodors Travel
