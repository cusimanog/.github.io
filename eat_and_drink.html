Sacha

This is the type of restaurant that makes everyone wish they were a regular, coming in for dinner every week and leaving in the wee hours. It offers the epitome of elevated comfort food: so-called “faux” ravioli stuffed with king crab, marinated oysters, bone marrow with a side of sirloin, and lots of wine. And if the owner Sacha Hormaechea is spotted greeting clients, make sure to shake his hand. He’s quite the character (Simons, 2012).
|32 minute bus ride from the Westin Palace Hotel|

Garcia de la Navarra

Garcia de la Navarra, a restaurant wine bar, family owned, is just a short walk from El Retiro Park. Here we offer 700 varieties of wines. Offering classic Spanish regions such as Ribera del Duero, Rioja, and Vega Sicilia Unico, some of which date back to the 1930’s. Each wine is carefully served at the right temperature. As you sip on one of these top wines, you will be delighted and satisfied with your choice of venue!
|11 minute walk or 4 minute taxi ride from the Westin Palace Hotel|

DiverXO

Enjoy the quality of Fine Dining Spanish fusion cuisine at DiverXO. Every dish is elegantly presented. Enjoy and experience delightful standout dishes including Pork skin with black brioche or salmon row and cherry sauce. DiverXO is known for winning the Spanish National Prize for Gastronomy and awarded three Michelin Stars (TimeOut, 2014). It is promised that every dish is guaranteed to satisfy the palate!!! This experience is more than just a frame of art as it also connects the different culinary traditions. Come and enjoy the quiet and friendly atmosphere DiverXO has to offer by making reservations!
|19 minute bus ride from the Westin Palace Hotel|
